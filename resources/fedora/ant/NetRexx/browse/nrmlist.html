The NetRexx mailing list is a place for discussing all aspects and implications of the NetRexx language.

Mailing lists ... in two paragraphs

A mailing list is a mailbox (for e-mail) that sends a copy of everything sent to it to everyone on a list of subscribers. Hence, if you subscribe to the mailing list, you get a copy of everything sent to the list; anything you send (post) to the list is sent to every subscriber.

So, if you have a question about NetRexx, first subscribe to the mailing list and then send your question to the mailing list. It's likely that someone will see the question and answer it; this answers your question, and also answers it for everyone else who might have the same question. This means everyone who subscribes learns from the questions being asked.

To subscribe to the NetRexx mailing list

  • Just send a piece of e-mail to majordomo@hursley.ibm.com with the text: 
      subscribe ibm-netrexx
    in the body of the mail.

To unsubscribe from the NetRexx mailing list

  • To remove your subscription, just send a piece of e-mail to majordomo@hursley.ibm.com with the text: 
      unsubscribe ibm-netrexx
    in the body of the mail.

To 'post' (send mail) to everyone on the NetRexx mailing list

  • Send your e-mail to ibm-netrexx@hursley.ibm.com.

    Please keep your posts short and to-the-point – you'll get better replies, that way!

The mailing list archives

  • ... can be found at http://ncc.hursley.ibm.com/majordomo/IBM-NETREXX/archives/

    Note: some recent archives may not be listed on the index page; these can be accessed using the same URL as ones which are listed, updating the year and month appropriately.

Problems with the mailing list?

  • Please send mail to the Hursley webmaster at www@hursley.ibm.com describing the problem in detail.

    Don't send mail about problems to the list .. or you'll likely just make the problem worse!
